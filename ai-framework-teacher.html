Teacher AI Competency Framework

Three progression levels (Foundation, Practitioner, Leader) across five domains. Click any cell to see the verbatim canonical descriptor. Use for self-assessment, professional development planning, and line-manager validation.

Eduversal AI Competency Framework v1.0 · Approved by Eduversal Academic Directorate · Companion playbook: Teacher Playbook

How to use this page

All teaching staff are expected to achieve Foundation level within the first year of framework implementation. Practitioner level reflects proficient AI integration. Leader level represents advanced expertise and is a specialist contribution — not all teachers are expected to reach it.

The matrix below shows what is expected at each level for each domain. Click a cell to read the verbatim descriptor. The descriptors are the source of truth for annual self-assessment.
