Eduversal AI Competency Framework v1.0 · Approved by Eduversal Academic Directorate

How to use this page

Every teacher should reach Foundation in the first year. Most teachers reach Practitioner after that. Leader is for teachers who help others — not everyone has to get there.

Click a box to read the exact words from the framework. The same window also shows what to try this week, what to never do, how to move to the next step, and which other pages to read.

Domain A
Knowledge & Understanding
Understands AI basics, key limitations (hallucination, bias), and ethical considerations. Knows the school's AI policy.

Deeper understanding of how LLMs work. Stays current with developments. Can explain AI to students and parents.

Comprehensive understanding. Engages with technical concepts and research literature. Anticipates future developments.

Domain B
Practical Application
Uses approved tools for lesson planning, materials creation, admin tasks. Reviews AI outputs before use. Follows data privacy.
Foundation · B
teacher.foundation.domainB
Advanced prompt engineering. Uses AI across differentiation, rubrics, feedback templates. Troubleshoots when outputs aren't right.
Practitioner · B
teacher.practitioner.domainB
Expert-level skills. Designs complex AI-enhanced workflows. Evaluates tools for school adoption. Develops resources for colleagues.
Leader · B
teacher.leader.domainB
Domain C
Pedagogical Integration
Uses AI materials to support learning objectives. Remains the instructional decision-maker. Can explain AI use to students.
Foundation · C
teacher.foundation.domainC
Integrates AI strategically for differentiation. Designs learning that leverages AI while maintaining rigour. Teaches students responsible use.
Practitioner · C
teacher.practitioner.domainC
Designs innovative AI-enhanced learning. Develops curriculum materials for the school/network. Mentors colleagues. Leads student AI literacy work.
Leader · C
teacher.leader.domainC
Domain D
Critical Evaluation
Fact-checks AI content. Spots obvious errors, false citations, cultural bias. Doesn't accept AI uncritically.
Foundation · D
teacher.foundation.domainD
Sophisticated evaluation — accuracy, depth, nuance, pedagogical fit. Spots subtle bias. Handles AI integrity concerns without false accusations.
Practitioner · D
teacher.practitioner.domainD
Contributes to school/network evaluation of tools, policies, integration outcomes. Leads ethical discussions. Advises leadership on emerging risks.
Leader · D
teacher.leader.domainD
Domain E
Professional Contribution
Engages with school AI initiatives. Shares basic tips with immediate colleagues. Asks for support when uncertain.
Foundation · E
teacher.foundation.domainE
Actively shares effective practices. Contributes to department/school discussions. Supports less experienced colleagues. Participates in PLCs.
Practitioner · E
teacher.practitioner.domainE
Leads PD sessions at school/network level. Contributes to AI policy development. Represents the school externally. Documents and shares effective practices.
