AI Competency Framework v1.0

Institutional AI Maturity Framework

School-level AI integration assessed across five maturity levels and six domains. Used by Eduversal Academic Directorate during school appraisal visits. Schools self-assess annually; the appraisal team validates.

Eduversal AI Competency Framework v1.0 · For Directors, Coordinators, Principals, Foundation Reps · Leader Playbook

How to use this page

The framework has 5 maturity levels (Emerging → Leading) across 6 domains. Each cell (5 × 6 = 30) describes what the school looks like at that level on that domain.

Most Eduversal schools in 2026 honestly sit at Level 1-2 on most domains. That is normal. Inflated self-assessment hurts twice — the appraisal team catches it, and the school sets targets it can't meet. Pick two domains per academic year to advance one level.

Maturity Levels at a Glance

The Five Levels

1
Emerging
Beginning to engage. Awareness limited. No systematic approach.
2
Developing
Initiated with basic policies. Inconsistent implementation. Building capabilities.
3
Established
Coherent integration. Majority staff engagement. Consistent practices.
4
Advanced
Sophisticated integration. Strong impact. Contributes to network learning.
5
Leading
Outstanding. Network model. Contributes to broader practice.

Assessment process

Step 1
School self-assesses
Annual self-assessment at the start of the academic year. Involves leadership team, department heads, representative teachers. Honest baseline.
Step 2
Network appraisal validates
Eduversal Academic Directorate appraisal visit reviews evidence, observes practice, interviews staff and students, validates or adjusts ratings.
Step 3
Benchmarking and reporting
Network-level annual report. Schools see their position relative to peers. Priorities identified for the coming year.

Phase 2-3 (planned): annual self-assessment writes to Firestore ai_maturity_assessments/{schoolId}_{academicYear}; Cloud Function maintains network-level aggregates; appraisal team validates from CH /ai-maturity-admin.
